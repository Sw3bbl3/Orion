# Welcome to Orion

Orion is a modern, high-performance Digital Audio Workstation (DAW) designed for musicians, producers, and developers who value speed, precision, and extensibility.

## The Orion Philosophy

Orion was built from the ground up to be more than just a recording tool. It is a creative environment that adapts to your workflow, whether you are scoring a film, producing an electronic masterpiece, or building custom audio extensions.

### Core Pillars

- **Performance**: Optimized for low-latency audio processing using our proprietary 64-bit engine (OAE).
- **Simplicity**: A clean, distraction-free interface that puts your music center stage.
- **Extensibility**: A powerful Lua-based extension system that allows you to build your own instruments and effects.
