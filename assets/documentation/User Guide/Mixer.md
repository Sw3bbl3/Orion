# The Mixer

The Mixer is where you balance and process your audio signals.

## Channel Strip
Each track in your project has a corresponding channel strip in the mixer.
- **Fader:** Controls the volume level.
- **Pan:** Controls the stereo position (Left/Right).
- **Mute (M):** Silences the track.
- **Solo (S):** Silences all other tracks.
- **Record Arm (R):** Prepares the track for recording.

## Inserts
The Insert Rack allows you to load VST/AU plugins or internal effects.
- Click an empty slot to add an effect.
- Drag effects to reorder them.
- Click the power button to bypass an effect.

## Sends
Use sends to route a portion of the signal to a Return Track (e.g., for Reverb or Delay).
- Adjust the send level using the knob.
- Create Return Tracks by right-clicking in the mixer.

## Master Channel
The Master Channel controls the overall output of your project. Effects placed here affect the entire mix.
Keep an eye on the master meter to ensure your signal doesn't clip (go above 0dB).
